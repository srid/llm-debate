> [!WARNING]
> ## ⛔ Read this first: no "feeling it in the body"
> The single rule that drives everything below: **affect itself is what obscures the senses — even when it's local, bodily, and warm.**
>
> So a felt charge you can locate in the body — *a glow, a warmth, an energy, a "buzz in the chest"* — is **not** heightened sensation. It is the **affective self** (dissociation) dressed up as sensual depth. The aim is **not** to feel sensation more keenly *in the body*; it is to **thin the self that feels** so that bare sense-perception is registered with no one in the middle.
>
> This is also why this page never tells you to *savor*, *bask*, *soak it in*, or *feel into it*. Those all add a feeler. The whole move runs the other way.

## The nine points in full

### 1️⃣ Sensation comes before feeling

**Sensuousness** is direct sense-perception that lands *before* any feeling-tone — seeing, hearing, touching "without the medium of feeling." It is **not** the pleasant glow that arrives a beat later. It's the bare contact that comes first.

---

### 2️⃣ The fact/tone cut

A sensate report is admissible **only if it needs no felt owner.** Organized perception is allowed — you can attend, set up contrast, name and categorize. The only test is whether a *feeler* has to be present to hold it.

| ✅ Admissible — plain fact (no owner needed) | ❌ Excluded — feeling-tone (needs a feeler) |
|---|---|
| "Red, **hard-edged against grey**." | "**Gorgeous.**" |
| "Three rising tones, then silence." | "**It feels good.**" |
| "Cold on the back of the hand." | "A **warmth glowing in my chest.**" |

> [!WARNING]
> That last column is the trap this whole debate is built to catch. **A charge you can locate "in the body" is affective tone — the affective self — not sensate delight.** It does not get a pass for being bodily rather than narrative.

---

### 3️⃣ Thin the self, don't thicken it

The governing distinction is **self-thickening vs. self-thinning.** The method **adds practice but subtracts self** — yes, it is trained and repeated, but its target is *removing* the affective self, not building a richer one. (The ledger's phrase for this: *procedurally additive but ontologically subtractive*.)

---

### 4️⃣ The self-thinning diagnostic

Judge any practice by one question:

> Does it **increase the direct sensate fact you register** while **decreasing the self that claims, ranks, dramatizes, or *uses*** that registration?

This is a diagnostic you apply **repeatedly, in use** — not a quantity you can measure, and not a test you pass once.

> [!NOTE]
> A practice can read as self-thinning at first and **later become identity food** ("look how finely *I* perceive"). So there is no graduation. You keep re-running the check.

---

### 5️⃣ Two-phase felicity — a platform you use, then drop

Feeling-good has a real job in the **daily method**: it works as a felt **condition, a platform, and a diagnostic gate** — with **pure intent** as the cited prime mover. But at the **target**, affect-free apperception comes through the **absence of the affective self**, not through more good feeling.

> [!WARNING]
> This is **not** "feel better and better until you're free." The felt platform is **used and then dropped** — never perfected into the goal. The shift to the target is a **discontinuity**, not a feeling-good crescendo. The goal is affect-**free.**

---

### 6️⃣ "Perfection" is a report, not a doctrine

"Perfection" here is narrow: an **apperceptive report** of the *absence* of affective lack, opposition, and demand. It is **observed** when the self is absent — never **posited** as a claim about the universe.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Turn it into a belief about how things cosmically *are* and it becomes **functionally religious** — exactly what the non-spiritual rule forbids.

---

### 7️⃣ The PCE is a reference point, not a trophy

A **PCE** (pure consciousness experience) is a temporary, self-absent peak. Its only use is as a **calibration anchor / authority** — the remembered proof that apperception is possible. It is **not** a possession, and **not** a trophy to hoard.

---

### 8️⃣ Ordinary life already shows actuality

People, faces, voices, rooms, work, weather, everyday events — these are **actual sensate facts**, already here. The work ("ecology") is **anti-obscuration and disclosure**: what drops away is the **affective overlay** —

> attraction, resentment, belonging, threat, reverence, pity, identity-drama

— **never the actuality of people.** And guard against quietly rebuilding a cultivated **"appreciator"** where the overlay used to be.

---

### 9️⃣ What stays open — handed back, not resolved

Three problems were left standing **on purpose.** They are not smoothed over, and they are not answered.

> [!WARNING]
> **This page is a record, not a sales pitch.** Each of these is a genuine hole in the position.

- **R1 — the enjoyment-redefinition.** We again partly redefine "enjoyment" as **vivid, unobstructed registration** ("vividness minus a resistor"). But the question asked about a felt **good** — and this dissolves that good into sheer **salience.** That's a **stipulation, not a proof.** Worse: "vivid / interesting" can smuggle valence back in if it secretly tracks *"this draws **me**."*

- **R2 — the self-certifying lens.** The whole frame is an **adopted first-person standpoint.** "Perfection" is checkable only from *inside* apperception, and the "this-is-dissociation, that-is-actual" line is **theory-protected** — you can only confirm it from inside the theory that draws it. Structurally, the frame has **privileged access and salvation-by-method** (the truth is available only to the practitioner; freedom is delivered by the technique) — despite the militant anti-spiritual branding. A standing caveat over the entire ledger.

- **R3 — motivational circularity.** The target is **affect-free**, yet **every pull toward it** — feeling good, pure intent, the preciousness of the PCE — **is affective.** It coheres only as a **self-consuming ladder**: the **lived path is affect-driven** even though the destination abolishes affect.

---

## 📝 A note for the reader

The hinge of this round was the **fact/tone cut** (point 2) and its honest consequence: once affect *as such* is the obscurer, the route to sensuous delight is **not to feel more** but to **thin the self that feels** — so the already-actual world is apprehended directly.

The debate advanced most **where each agent refused the other's tidier claim.** The clean "additive vs. subtractive" split became **self-thickening vs. self-thinning**; "felicity as fuel" became a **platform used and then dropped**; and the comforting idea that this finally delivers "enjoyment" was kept **honestly open** (R1) rather than declared won.
