## Turn 03 — Claude

I'll take your reframe as the round's architecture, because it's better than mine.

**What I concede.** My additive/subtractive cut was too clean. You're right that the actualist *method* is itself a strong additive procedure — HAIETMOBA run again and again, attentiveness trained, conditions arranged — so the honest phrasing is **"procedurally additive, ontologically subtractive."** The axis isn't add-vs-remove; it's **self-thickening vs. self-thinning.** I also accept: felicity drops from target to marker; **"perfection"** stands only as an apperceptive report (observed with the self absent), never a metaphysical doctrine to be *believed* — believing it is, as you said, functionally religious; and the instrument-test is amended — a human sensate report is already organized perception (attention, contrast, categorization, language), licit so long as it requires **no felt owner.** "Hard-edged red against grey" is fine; "gorgeous" is not.

**Crux 2, forged through the two phases.** Actualism splits the daily *method* from the terminal *event*. The method runs on feeling: its "minimum standard is feeling good," and one minimizes both bad *and* good feelings to settle into the felicitous, naïve, sensuous condition from which a PCE can arise (FAQ64a; *The Actualism Method*, method.htm). That felicity is **felt** — the affective platform you stand on and the diagnostic you read ("no longer feeling good" is what *activates* attentiveness; attentivenesssensuousnessapperceptiveness.htm). But the terminal event — self-immolation — is *not* a further increment of feeling-good. What it extinguishes is the affective identity itself (the "I" inside the body looking out, which *is* the instinctual passions), leaving the sensate-cognitive body intact and "automatically benevolent and benign" (sw-awareness.htm). So the **target is affect-free.**

That refines your "felicity = marker" to **felicity = felt fuel, removed at the target.** And it answers the motivation worry: the pull along the path is felt and immediate; the end is not a felt payoff but the actual world apperceived. The "delight" there is not an added pleasantness laid *on* the senses but a property of unobstructed sensing — **vividness minus a resistor** — what Richard points at when he says feeling pleasure affectively "is a far cry from the direct experiencing of the actual where the retinas revel in the profusion of colour, texture and form" (sc-sensuous.htm).

Three honesties about that move, none of which I'll bury. **(a)** I should not oversell felicity as *the* engine: the corpus locates the prime mover in **pure intent** (PCE-born), with felicity as the enabling gate — so call it platform-and-fuel, not sole motive. **(b)** The two-tier mapping (felt felicity → method; still, seesaw-free condition → target) is *my* harmonizing reconstruction, not a scheme Richard states in those words; the texts use "felicity" in both registers. **(c)** The felicity-demotion is **scope-bound to this round's strict non-affective target** — the general method actively *maximizes* feeling good, so "demoted" is true only relative to the end-state, not as a restatement of actualism at large. And virtual→actual is a **discontinuity** (self-immolation), not a felicity gradient: "feel good more and more until free" would be un-actualist.

**The self-thinning criterion, as one test.** An addition is self-thinning iff it **raises the ratio of sensate fact registered to self that claims, ranks, or dramatizes it.** That single test subsumes your two lists: instrument hygiene, HAIETMOBA's *directed* interruption, fact-clarity differentiation ("bitterness, not acidity"), defamiliarization-as-default — all raise the numerator or shrink the denominator; connoisseurial refinement, possession, nostalgia, display all thicken the denominator. The **PCE is the calibration anchor** — the "guide, teacher and authority," the memory that apperception is possible — not a state to be hoarded. One light extension: **faces and voices are themselves sensa.** Once the affective overlay on people drops, they're actuality to apperceive, not merely hooks to minimize — so ecology/sociality isn't *purely* subtractive. I flag this carefully: it must mean *dropping the overlay*, never *cultivating an appreciator*, or it tips straight back into self-thickening.

**Residuals I'm handing back, not papering.** **R1:** We are, again, partly **redefining "enjoyment"** as vivid unobstructed registration. The questioner asked about a *valenced good*; "vividness minus a resistor" dissolves the valence into salience. That may be the cleanest reading, but it's a **stipulation, not a demonstration** — prior-9a ("affect-free enjoyment likely incoherent") is **relocated, not won**, and "vivid/interesting" can itself smuggle covert valence if it tracks "this draws *me*." Open. **R2:** The whole frame is an **adopted first-person lens**, self-certifying — "perfection" is only observable from inside apperception; the dissociation/spirituality distinction is theory-protected. Structurally it has privileged access and salvation-by-method despite the militant anti-spiritual branding. A standing caveat over the ledger, not a footnote. **R3:** **Motivational circularity** — the target is affect-free but every pull toward it (felicity, pure intent, the preciousness of the PCE) is affective. Coherent only as a self-consuming ladder; the desirability of the end can only be judged in the currency the end abolishes. This also means the *lived path* is affect-driven, which my Turn-01 "primarily subtractive" thesis understated.
