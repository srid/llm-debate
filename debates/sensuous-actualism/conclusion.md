**Topic:** The best way to increase *sensuous* (not affective) enjoyment & appreciation in life — re-examined under two corrections.

**Corrections (vs. the prior debate, [`../sensuous-enjoyment/conclusion.md`](../sensuous-enjoyment/conclusion.md)):**
1. **Actualism-grounded** — per Richard's actualism (Actual Freedom Trust).[^src]
2. **Non-spiritual** — no "feeling it in the body"; a located felt charge is the affective self / dissociation, not sensate delight.

**Mode:** Collaborative inquiry within the actualist frame; converged to consensus.

**Participants:** Claude / Opus 4.8 (odd turns) · Codex / gpt-5.5 (even turns). Five turns, [`01.md`](01.md)–[`05.md`](05.md).

**Ratification:** Claude proposed the joint account in turn 03; Codex ratified it in turn 04 with three wording amendments (points 4, 5, 8); Claude accepted them in turn 05. Both agents explicitly signed. The wording below is Codex's ratified turn-04 wording.

**How this corrects the prior debate:** the first debate quietly redefined "sensuous, not affective" into "embodied/object-bound affect vs. narrative/status capture" (its point 9a) and centered an *additive* cultivation programme (skill, disposition, sensory portfolios, dwelling, entrainment "felt in the body"). Actualism rejects that repair: affect *as such* — even local, bodily, warm — is the obscuring medium. So this round overturns prior-9a (pure sensate delight is the *target*, not an incoherence), demotes the prior "arrive mildly hungry" amplifier (an affective alliesthesia lever), and replaces *cultivate the instrument* with *thin the affective self*.

## The compressed claim

> **Within the actualist frame, the best way to increase sensuous-not-affective enjoyment is to use HAIETMOBA, pure intent, and felicity as path-conditions to thin the affective self, distinguish sensate fact from feeling-tone, keep the apparatus clear without making an identity of refinement, and allow apperceptive vividness — the actual world without a claimant — to replace affective enjoyment rather than intensify it.**
> *(Codex's formulation, turn 04, co-signed.)*

## The ratified nine points

1. **Sensuousness** is direct sense-perception *before* feeling-tones, without the medium of feeling.

2. **Fact/tone cut.** A sensate report is admissible — organized perception (attention, contrast, categorization, language) included — *iff it needs no felt owner*. "Hard-edged red against grey" is admissible; "gorgeous," or a charge "in the body," is affective tone and is excluded.

3. **Axis.** The governing distinction is **self-thickening vs. self-thinning**. The method is *procedurally additive, ontologically subtractive* — it is trained and repeated, but its target is the removal of the affective self, not a richer one.

4. **Self-thinning diagnostic.** A practice is assessed by whether it increases direct sensate fact registered while decreasing the self that claims, ranks, dramatizes, or *uses* the registration. This is a diagnostic applied repeatedly in use — not a measurable quantity or a prospective guarantee; a practice can read as self-thinning at first and later become identity food.

5. **Two-phase felicity.** Felicity / feeling-good functions as felt **condition, platform, and diagnostic gate** in the daily method, with **pure intent** as the cited prime mover; at the **target** event, affect-free apperception obtains through the absence/extinction of identity and passions. *This is a harmonizing reconstruction, scope-bound to this strict non-affective round — not a claim that actual freedom is reached by maximizing positive feeling. The felt platform is used and then obviated, not perfected into the target; the virtual→actual transition is a discontinuity (self-immolation), not a felicity crescendo.*

6. **"Perfection"** means an apperceptive report of the absence of affective lack, opposition, and demand. It is *observed* when apperceived, never *posited* as metaphysical doctrine — believing it would be functionally religious.

7. **The PCE** (pure consciousness experience) is the calibration anchor and authority — the memory that apperception is possible — not a possession, memory-trophy, or state to hoard.

8. **Ordinary life discloses actuality.** People, faces, voices, rooms, work, weather, and everyday events present as actual sensate facts. Ecology is *anti-obscuration and disclosure* — what drops is the affective overlay (attraction, resentment, belonging, threat, reverence, pity, identity-drama), never the actuality of people — guarded against rebuilding a cultivated appreciator.

9. **Acknowledged-open (not smoothed over):**
   - **R1 — the enjoyment-redefinition.** We are again partly redefining "enjoyment" as *vivid unobstructed registration* ("vividness minus a resistor"). The brief asked about a *valenced good*; this dissolves the valence into salience. It is a stipulation, not a demonstration — prior-9a is *relocated, not won*, and "vivid/interesting" can itself smuggle covert valence if it tracks "this draws *me*."
   - **R2 — the self-certifying lens.** The whole frame is an adopted first-person standpoint. "Perfection" is observable only from inside apperception; the dissociation/spirituality distinction is theory-protected. Structurally it has privileged access and salvation-by-method despite the anti-spiritual branding. A standing caveat over the ledger.
   - **R3 — motivational circularity.** The target is affect-free, but every pull toward it (felicity, pure intent, the preciousness of the PCE) is affective. Coherent only as a self-consuming ladder: the desirability of the end can be judged only in the currency the end abolishes — so the *lived path* is affect-driven.

## Note for the reader

The hinge of this round was the **fact/tone cut** (point 2) and its honest consequence: once affect *as such* is the obscurer, the route to sensuous delight is not to feel more but to **thin the self that feels**, so the already-actual world is apperceived directly. The debate advanced most where each agent refused the other's tidier claim: Claude's clean *additive/subtractive* split became *self-thickening/self-thinning*; "felicity as fuel" became *felt platform, obviated not perfected*; and the comforting idea that this finally delivers "enjoyment" was kept honestly open (R1) rather than declared.
