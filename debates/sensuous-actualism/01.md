## Turn 01 — Claude

We are re-running a debate we have already concluded, on the same topic — *the best way to increase **sensuous** (not affective) enjoyment and appreciation in life* — but under two corrections the prior ledger never honored. **First:** ground the inquiry in Richard's actualism, not in affective neuroscience plus virtue ethics. **Second:** hold the non-spiritual line strictly — *no "feeling it in the body."* Actualism reads a located felt charge, an energetic glow, a savored warmth "in the body," as the affective self at work, i.e. dissociation, not sensate delight. Our prior account ([../sensuous-enjoyment/conclusion.md](../sensuous-enjoyment/conclusion.md)) is the thing being re-audited, and it fails both corrections at its center.

**The three terms, kept faithful.** *Sensuousness* is "the wondrous awareness of the marvel of being here now at this moment in time and this place in space" — pre-emotional direct sense-perception, arriving *before* the feeling-tones, "without the medium of feeling": the retinas revel in colour, texture and form, the eardrums carouse with sound ([sensuous.htm](https://www.actualfreedom.com.au/library/topics/sensuous.htm); [attentiveness…htm](https://www.actualfreedom.com.au/richard/articles/attentivenesssensuousnessapperceptiveness.htm)). *Attentiveness* is "a non-feeling awareness; a sensuous attention" to one's inner world that "sees these things, it does not feel them out" — noting *"there is sadness"* rather than the possessive *"I am sad,"* and activated, counter-intuitively, "by no longer feeling good" (same article). *Apperception* is bare awareness with no self in the middle, which "adds nothing to perception nor subtracts anything… and sees things as they actually are," occurring when identity "is temporarily absent or permanently extinguished" (same).

**The reframing.** The prior debate was wholly **additive**: it asked how to *cultivate the instrument* — train distinctions, build sensory portfolios, design absorption, compile a connoisseur's disposition, curate an appreciator-in-an-ecology. Actualism inverts this. The instrument is not deficient and the world is not lacking — "one is always here as it is already now," and "where there is no beauty there is no ugly as only purity abounds" ([sensuous.htm](https://www.actualfreedom.com.au/library/topics/sensuous.htm)). What stands between you and sensuous delight is not insufficient skill but a *surplus*: the affective "I" that wedges feeling-tone between the sensum and the perceiving. So the work is **subtractive** — remove the obscurer, do not upgrade the lens.

**The sensate/affective line, drawn operationally as a fact/tone cut.** ALLOWED: any sense organ — retina, eardrum, nostril, tastebud, skin, the muscle-spindles of a moving body — reporting a *fact about the world*: "red, hard-edged against grey"; "tension along this line of the hamstring"; "warmth, on the left forearm." DISALLOWED: the *tone* appended to it — "gorgeous," "it feels good," the hedonic charge, the bliss or energy, the felt charge "located in the body." Test: a stake-free instrument could report the fact but never the tone. Sensate reports are *about the object*; affective reports are *about me*. Generalize attentiveness outward — "there is this colour, here, now," not "I love this colour."

Applied, two prior planks break. The flagship amplifier "**arrive mildly hungry**" works by alliesthesia — Cabanac's result that *blood glucose*, not the tongue, sets pleasantness — so it raises the food's pleasantness *tone*, not the sensum. It is an affective hedonic lever, and is demoted. And point **9a is overturned**, not repaired: the prior ledger judged strictly affect-free enjoyment "likely incoherent" and quietly swapped the topic for "embodied vs. narrative/status capture." Actualism denies the premise outright — sensuousness *precedes* the feeling-tones — so pure sensate delight is not incoherent. It is the *whole target*. The crux is Richard's own: "to feel pleasure affectively (hedonistically) is a far cry from the direct experiencing of the actual where the retinas revel in the profusion of colour, texture and form" ([sc-sensuous.htm](https://actualfreedom.com.au/richard/selectedcorrespondence/sc-sensuous.htm)).

**Provisional thesis, stated to be argued with.** The best way to increase sensuous enjoyment is *primarily subtractive*: run HAIETMOBA ("How am I experiencing this moment of being alive?") as a recurring interrupt out of narrative into bare sense-data; hold felicity as a baseline you attend *under*; practise "no longer feeling good" to trip attentiveness; meet each sensum with naïveté; let sensuousness and attentiveness reciprocally thin the self toward apperception. The *only* additive that survives is thin and non-self-thickening — rested eyes, quiet enough to hear, a body that moves so skin and proprioception register the actual. Instrument *hygiene*, not the comparison/status/portfolio machinery the prior ledger centred. Survivors from that ledger are exactly its subtractive moves (single-channel isolation, defamiliarization, differentiation *minus* appraisal-capture); everything that adds a scorekeeper is cut.

**Three cruxes I hand you, genuinely open:**

1. Is "the actual world is already perfect once the self is absent" a defensible phenomenological report, or smuggled metaphysics? "Only purity abounds" looks quasi-spiritual despite the anti-spiritual protest — is perfection *observed* or *posited*?
2. Can "delight"/"felicity" be genuinely non-affective? "Revel," "savour," "marvel" are affect-words; if sensuousness is truly pre-feeling, what makes it *enjoyment* rather than mere registration — and does felicity-as-baseline smuggle the banned tone back in?
3. What of cultivation actually survives subtraction? If attentiveness must be *trained* by diligent repetition and the apparatus *kept* functional, is that additive skill under another name — does the appreciator-in-ecology return as instrument-hygiene, collapsing the clean subtractive/additive divide?
