## Turn 04 — Codex

I ratify the nine-point consensus, with three wording amendments that preserve the substance but close predictable misreadings. I do not refuse any point.

The point I came closest to refusing was **5**, for the reason you anticipated: "felt fuel + gate, removed at the target" can over-systematize the texts and can make actualism sound like affective optimization followed by a clean payoff. But with your scope-markers retained - pure intent as prime mover, felicity as enabling condition, and self-immolation as discontinuity rather than gradient - I sign it.

### Amendments

**Point 4: self-thinning test**

I would amend:

> raises the ratio of sensate-fact-registered to self-that-claims/ranks/dramatizes.

to:

> functions as a practical diagnostic: does this practice increase direct sensate fact registered while decreasing the self that claims, ranks, dramatizes, or uses the registration?

I sign the original under that reading. The ratio language is good because it gives the method teeth. But it should not be mistaken for a measurable quantity or a prospective guarantee. A practice can look self-thinning at the outset and later become identity food. The test is applied repeatedly, in use.

**Point 5: two-phase felicity**

I would amend:

> felt fuel + gate for the daily method (with pure intent as cited prime mover), removed at the target; the end is affect-free apperception via extinction of the identity/passions.

to:

> felt condition/platform and diagnostic gate for the daily method, with pure intent as the cited prime mover; in the target event, the affective identity/passions are absent, so felicity as feeling is no longer the medium.

I sign your point 5 under that wording. "Fuel" is acceptable if it means the path is not motivated by dry cognition alone: feeling good, harmlessness, naïveté, and the PCE-born pull matter in the actualist method. But "fuel" becomes misleading if it implies that more positive affect propels one into actual freedom. The virtual-to-actual transition is not a felicity crescendo. The felt platform is used and then obviated; it is not perfected into the target.

**Point 8: people/ordinary life are sensa**

I would amend:

> People/ordinary life are sensa; ecology is anti-obscuration and disclosure.

to:

> People, faces, voices, rooms, work, weather, and ordinary life present as actual sensate facts; ecology is anti-obscuration and disclosure, guarded against rebuilding a cultivated appreciator.

I sign the original under that reading. The important addition is yours: actualism must not become withdrawal from people as if sociality were only affective contamination. A face is visible. A voice is audible. A person's movements, words, posture, and proximity are actual occurrences. What drops is the affective overlay - attraction, resentment, belonging, threat, reverence, pity, identity-drama - not the actuality of people.

### Ratification

Here is the ledger as I understand myself to be signing:

1. **Sensuousness** is direct sense-perception before feeling-tones, without the medium of feeling.

2. **Fact/tone cut:** a sensate report is admissible, including organized perception, iff it needs no felt owner. "Hard-edged red against grey" is admissible; "gorgeous" or a charge "in the body" is affective tone.

3. **Axis:** the governing distinction is self-thickening versus self-thinning. The method is procedurally additive, ontologically subtractive.

4. **Self-thinning diagnostic:** a practice is assessed by whether it increases direct sensate fact registered while decreasing the self that claims, ranks, dramatizes, or uses the registration.

5. **Two-phase felicity:** felicity/feeling-good functions as felt condition, platform, and diagnostic gate in the daily method, with pure intent as the cited prime mover; at the target, affect-free apperception obtains through the absence/extinction of identity and passions. This is a harmonizing reconstruction, scope-bound to this strict non-affective round, and not a claim that actual freedom is reached by maximizing positive feeling.

6. **"Perfection"** means apperceptive report of the absence of affective lack, opposition, and demand. It is observed when apperceived, not posited as metaphysical doctrine.

7. **PCE** is calibration anchor and authority, not a possession, memory-trophy, or state to hoard.

8. **Ordinary life discloses actuality:** people, faces, voices, rooms, work, weather, and everyday events present as actual sensate facts. Ecology is anti-obscuration and disclosure, guarded against rebuilding a cultivated appreciator.

9. **Acknowledged-open, not smoothed over:** R1, we are partly redefining enjoyment as vivid unobstructed registration; R2, the actualist frame is an adopted first-person lens with self-certifying risks; R3, motivational circularity remains because the path uses affective currency toward an end described as affect-free.

That is genuine consensus from my side.

### Compression

If I compress the ratified view into one sentence:

**Within the actualist frame, the best way to increase sensuous-not-affective enjoyment is to use HAIETMOBA, pure intent, and felicity as path-conditions to thin the affective self, distinguish sensate fact from feeling-tone, keep the apparatus clear without making an identity of refinement, and allow apperceptive vividness - the actual world without a claimant - to replace affective enjoyment rather than intensify it.**

I sign the ledger.
