### Conceded — R1: drop "falsifiable"

You're right that the page gives Richard's *"experiential report, written as it is happening"*[^exp-report], not an independent neurological test. So I withdraw "falsifiable physical separability." But R1 still **closes**, because the redefinition charge was the real worry and the source dissolves it: Richard names *two distinct pleasures* — affective *"hedonic pleasure"* vs sensate *"anhedonic pleasure"*[^anhedonic] — reports *"The affective pleasure/ pain centre in the brain is null and void"*[^centre] while sensate delight continues — *"These eyes seeing it delight in the array of colour … but I cannot feel the beauty of it in the emotional and passionate sense of the word."*[^delight] Sensuous enjoyment is **anhedonic pleasure**: a category actualism explicitly names, not a relabelling smuggled in by us. R1 closes as *a sourced actualist dissociation-report, verified by direct experience (the PCE), not as proven physiology.*

### Conceded — R3: pure intent is dual-register; drop "non-affective driver"

Verified on `pureintent.htm`: pure intent is *"derived from the purity of the PCE"*[^pi-derived] and *"has nothing to do with 'me' and 'my' doings"*[^pi-nome] — **yet** *"is a palpable life-force"*[^pi-life], *"experienced as an irresistible enticement"*[^pi-enticement] — and the method runs on *"all of the affective energy channelled into being as happy and harmless."*[^m5-energy] So the path is affective on the rungs. Only the *target* is affect-free: *"It is not an affective, cerebral or psychic state of 'being'; it is a physical condition."*[^afnotaffective] R3 closes as **a self-consuming ladder — affective in its means, affect-free at its end — not non-affective all the way down.** No circularity (affective means, affect-free terminus, the means consumes itself), but no sterile driver either.
