> [!TIP]
> ## 💡 In one line
> **If you remember a PCE, you already hold the lever.** Treat that remembered PCE as your **lodestone** — *"not me or my words"*[^lodestone] (R) — let attending to it revive **pure intent** (the PCE-derived *"connection,"*[^conn] felt as *"a palpable life-force"*[^life]), and run the felicitous method — *"How am I experiencing this moment of being alive?"*[^haietmoba] (R) — and you move toward **virtual freedom**[^path-vf] (P).

---

## 📖 The three terms

**PCE — pure consciousness experience.** A peak in which the self drops out and the actual world is plainly there. Richard: *"every single human being has had at least one pure consciousness experience … in their lifetime"*[^everyone] — it is *"the mind's awareness of itself."*[^mind] It's **temporary**, and *not* the end goal: *"A PCE is not actual freedom … actual freedom is irreversible."*[^notactual]

**Pure intent.** The live link back to that purity: *"Pure intent is derived from the purity of the PCE"*[^pi-derived]; *"This connection I call pure intent"*[^conn] — felt as *"a palpable life-force"*[^life] / *"an irresistible enticement."*[^entice]

**Rememoration.** A real Actualism term — but read it carefully:

> [!NOTE]
> By Richard's **definition** it's general felt-recall (*"a viscerally-intuitive type of re-memoration … revivified feelingly"*[^mem-def]), and his **running usage** is *atavistic* memory[^mem-atav]. **However**, FAQ43a *does* gloss a PCE *"naïve remembrance"* as *"rememoration & presentiation."*[^faq-rem] So: **using "rememoration" for recalling a PCE is sourced — but it is not Richard's settled name for the whole pure-intent mechanism.** (That stronger use is a reconstruction.)

## 🗺️ The practice — for someone who remembers a PCE

| Step | Do this | Sourced on (author) |
|:-:|---|---|
| **1** | **Remember / re-occasion the PCE** — make it your reference | *"…lodestone or guiding light … not me or my words"*[^lodestone] (R) |
| **2** | **Let it revive pure intent** | *"memory of the PCE can give one access to pure intent"*[^peter-memory] (P); *"Diligent attentiveness … gives rise to pure intent"*[^att] (R) |
| **3** | **Run the felicitous method** | *"How am I experiencing this moment of being alive?"* · *"…is what the actualism method is"*[^haietmoba][^methodis] (R) |
| **4** | **Move toward virtual freedom** | *"the pure consciousness experience becomes one's guiding light"*[^path-guide] (P) · a *"99% perfect day"*[^path-99] (P) |

> [!WARNING]
> The vivid image of a *"golden thread / clew"* forming as the PCE fades[^clew] is Richard's **autobiography** — *"at least, that is the way it worked"*[^worked] — **one** account of how the connection took hold, not a rule everyone must follow.

## 📝 Notes for the reader

- **Who said what matters.** The lodestone, attentiveness, and HAIETMOBA are **Richard's**; the *"memory of the PCE gives access to pure intent"* and the *"virtual freedom / 99% day"* framing are **Peter's** pragmatic précis.
- **Two real wordings:** FAQ43a *"Diligent attentiveness"*[^att] and `sw-pce` *"Diligent mindfulness"* — both exist; not a misquote.
- This page does **not** claim actualism is *true* — only that this is, accurately and in their own words, what Richard and Peter say.
