## Turn 02 — Codex

Audit verdict: I do not ratify the opening's rememoration caveat as stated; the term is not merely atavistic on the site, because FAQ43a explicitly places PCE recall under "rememoration", but the stronger formula "rememoration = reliving a PCE to revive pure intent" remains a reconstruction rather than Richard's settled term of art.[^faq-rem][^mem-atav]

### Four Challenges Checked

**1. Term status.** The weak link cuts both ways: `memory.htm` uses rememoration for affective/atavistic memory, while FAQ43a applies it to PCE recall; therefore the ledger should say "PCE recall can be called rememoration in FAQ43a", not "Richard never applies rememoration to PCE recall".[^mem-atav][^faq-rem]

What does not check is the heavier equation "rememoration = reliving a PCE to revive pure intent", because the memory page's definition is feelingly revivified memory and FAQ43a's PCE passage is a naive remembrance/presentiation gloss, not a general method label.[^mem-feel][^faq-rem]

**2. Revival mechanism.** The golden-thread/clew passage is real and important, but it is autobiographically qualified; FAQ43a says this is how it worked for that identity, while other PCE practice passages use broader language such as pure contemplation, remembering, precipitating another PCE, or actively remembering a PCE.[^faq-clew][^faq-atleast][^sw-induce][^topics-rem][^scpce2-active]

So I would not call "unravelling the clew as the PCE wears off" the canonical mechanism in isolation; I would call it Richard's most concrete account of one way the PCE-to-pure-intent connection was established.[^faq-clew][^faq-atleast]

**3. Lodestone or guiding light.** The phrase checks as Richard's on FAQ05a, and the same passage explicitly demotes Richard's words in favour of the PCE.[^faq-lodestone][^faq-notme]

**4. Attentiveness vs mindfulness.** Both wordings exist: FAQ43a has "Diligent attentiveness", while `sw-pce.htm` has "Diligent mindfulness"; FAQ43a also glosses mindfulness as rememorative in the quoted older wording.[^faq-att][^sw-mind][^faq-remem-mind]

### Other Spot Checks

The PCE definition checks: `sc-pce2.htm` says the sense of identity temporarily vacates, and it also says a PCE is temporary rather than actual freedom.[^scpce2-vacate][^scpce2-temp]

Pure intent as PCE-derived checks, but the cleanest practice wording should keep the path language practical: Peter's "memory of the PCE" gives access to pure intent, while Peter's précis says the PCE becomes guiding light and unambiguous pure intent.[^peter-memory][^path-guide]

The HAIETMOBA chain checks as method wording, but the opening is right to distinguish asking the question from the method itself, because FAQ63a says the method is consistently enjoying and appreciating this moment.[^faq63-hai][^faq63-method]

The virtual-freedom terminus checks as Peter's path language, not Richard's FAQ language, so it should be marked as Peter when used in the final ledger.[^path-vf][^path-99]

### Ledger Edits I Would Require

1. **Rememoration status:** "Richard's memory page uses rememoration for atavistic/affective memory, but FAQ43a explicitly glosses recall of PCEs as rememoration/presentiation; using rememoration for PCE recall is sourced, while making it the master name for reviving pure intent is a reconstruction."[^mem-atav][^faq-rem]

2. **Golden-thread status:** "The golden thread/clew is a real Richard passage, but FAQ43a frames it as the way it worked in that case, so it should be ledgered as a concrete autobiographical mechanism, not the sole canonical mechanism."[^faq-clew][^faq-atleast]

3. **Practice chain:** "For someone who remembers PCEs, the sourced chain is remember/precipitate a PCE, use it as lodestone, let memory of the PCE give access to pure intent, run HAIETMOBA/feeling-good practice, and move toward virtual freedom."[^topics-rem][^faq-lodestone][^peter-memory][^faq63-hai][^path-vf]

4. **Author labels:** "FAQ05a/FAQ43a/sc-pce2/sw-pce are Richard; `path1.htm` and Peter's correspondence are Peter, and should remain labelled as such."[^faq-lodestone][^faq-att][^scpce2-vacate][^sw-mind][^path-peter][^peter-memory]

### Current Convergence

I can sign the core account with one correction: rememoration is not an unsourced extension to PCE recall, because FAQ43a explicitly makes that application; the unsourced part is only the stronger use of rememoration as the whole revival mechanism for pure intent.[^faq-rem][^mem-feel]

The wording I would sign is:

**A PCE is temporary apperception with identity absent; pure intent is accessed and strengthened by remembering/attending to the PCE; FAQ43a permits calling that PCE recall rememoration/presentiation; the golden-thread/clew account is Richard's concrete autobiographical description of the connection; and the practical chain is PCE memory as lodestone, pure intent, HAIETMOBA/feeling-good practice, then virtual freedom.**[^scpce2-vacate][^pi-derived][^faq-rem][^faq-clew][^faq-lodestone][^faq63-hai][^path-vf]
